AMENITIES

An Array Of Amenities And Services

Hotel Cardoso Haiti blends warm, Haitian hospitality with all of the amenities of a boutique hotel. As our guest, you will enjoy an array of amenities and services. From free perks like Wi-Fi, parking, daily housekeeping and a hot breakfast buffet to an outdoor pool, onsite guest laundry and fitness center, we’re equipped with everything you need for a relaxing, rewarding stay in Petion-Ville.

FREE WIFI

We offer free Wi-Fi. For an extra charge, you can also choose the high-speed Internet.

FREE BREAKFAST BUFFET

We offer free continental breakfast to all in-house guests along with warm Haitian Breakfast Savories.

FREE DAILY HOUSEKEEPING SERVICE

Daily housekeeping service

COMPLIMENTARY PARKING

Enjoy our onsite parking.

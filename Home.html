Welcome to Hotel cardoso Haiti

Your Home Away From Home

Find your home away from home at Hotel Cardoso Haiti. Since 1974, our family run hotel has offered business and leisure guests a refined, relaxing getaway in close proximity to the Delmas suburb. Located at the top of the hill, our 80-room hotel is framed by gardens and panoramic views of Petion-Ville, Port-au-Prince Bay and the surrounding mountains. Our Art Deco design welcomes in the beauty of our tropical surroundings with bright, open spaces, oversized windows and terraces overlooking the gardens. At every moment of your stay, you will enjoy warm, authentic Haitian hospitality from staff members who welcome each guest as family.

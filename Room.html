Hospitality & Comfort Like No Other

Among hotels in Port-au-Prince, Hotel Cardoso Haiti brings together warm Haitian hospitality and contemporary comfort like no other destination. Choose from among 80 accommodations, including 60 beautifully appointed guest rooms and 20 suites. All have been individually designed, uniquely decorated and equipped with the full range of personal comforts and amenities. What they share in common are authentic Haitian accessories, Art Deco furnishings, artwork and fabrics. All suites and superior rooms have been designed for short-term and long-term stays. They each have their own kitchenette or kitchen and a private balcony or terrace. Complimentary breakfast buffet and Internet access are offered in all room types.
